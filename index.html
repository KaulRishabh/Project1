Charactersketch of Rishabh Kaul.

  • Nature Loving

    He loved nature very much because he would love a walk early in the morning through the dewy fields and loved watching birds tweet.

  • Caring

    He was very caring because when he found a baby rabbit unconcious in the fields he brought it home to take care of it.

  • Cheerful

    He was a very cheerful man because when the moher said that the rabbit woul not survive he told her that it would keep living.

  • Hard Working

    He was a hardworking man as he used to work in the pit where he used to mine coal the whole night.

  • Optimistic

    He was an optimistic man because when the children asked him if the rabbit would survive he said yes it would.
