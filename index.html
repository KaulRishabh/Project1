Beauty Of Birds.

  • Peacock

    They have beutiful feathers and they are the national birds of India.

  • Eagle

    The eagle is a big bird with a grip strength 10 times stronger than that of humans.

  • Parrot

    Parrots are beautiful birds which have more than 390 species and they can imitate human voices thus making them a vey popular pet.

  • Ostrich

    Ostriches are the largest birds on the planet , they are flightless and have 3 stomachs.

  • Owl

    Owls are night predators , have flexible necks and they can see well at night but not during the day.
